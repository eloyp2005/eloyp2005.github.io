My Everyday Life

I attend Coral Gables High School 5 times a week for studies. I wake up at 6:20 A.M. and have to go to school. I leave school at 2:20 P.M. and then go straight home. Simle process but very tiring. I get home and usually have about an hour - hour and a half of homework and then just have enough time to chill at home until my parents get home. When my parents get home, I usually just eat, shower, sometimes watch a movie with them and then just go to sleep and then the process repeats itself over and over.
