My Academy: BMIT

Info About It

The program focuses on transferable skills and emphasizes the logical order of establishing and growing a business. Technology and accounting procedures used in the work place are essential components of tomorrow's workforce demands. With over 175 multinational companies located in the city of Coral Gables, the academy networks with firms to establish internships and work sites for students. Graduates emerge from the academy with integrity and evolve into individuals capable of success in a competitive, ever-changing global community.
